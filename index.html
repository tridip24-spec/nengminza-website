About Us · Leadership

A Vision Born in the Hills of Garo

Nengminza Enterprises is more than a business — it is the materialisation of one person's conviction that enterprise, when rooted in community, has the power to transform a region. Founded and led from Garo Hills, the company works at the crossroads of agriculture, rural infrastructure, and government-backed development.

From rubber plantations spanning 18 years to MNREGA supply operations covering 4 villages across 4+ districts, every venture is built with a single purpose: to create lasting economic foundations for the people of Meghalaya.

Lovestone Nengminza Sangma
Founder & Managing Director

Where legal precision meets entrepreneurial vision, Lovestone Nengminza Sangma stands apart. A law graduate by discipline and a builder by nature, he brings an uncommon combination of analytical rigour and bold strategic instinct to everything he leads.

"Business, when executed with integrity, is the most powerful force for change a region can have."

Born and raised in the Garo Hills of Meghalaya, his leadership is shaped by something no boardroom can teach — a deep, lived understanding of the land, its people, and its untapped promise. That closeness to home isn't sentiment. It's strategy.

His legal foundation gives Nengminza Enterprises its backbone — a sharp framework for navigating government contracts, regulatory complexity, and rural development policy. Under his leadership, the enterprise operates where it matters most: at the intersection of economic growth and lasting community impact.

What We Do

Our Business Ventures

Four strategic pillars driving growth and impact across Meghalaya.

🌳

Rubber Plantation

A deliberate move into Meghalaya's emerging rubber sector — contributing to India's push for higher domestic rubber output, built on 18 years of plantation expertise.

  • Location: Era-Aning Village, South Garo Hills
  • Area: 6.7 acres
  • Trees: 2,700 mature rubber trees
  • Production: 10 tons latex annually
  • Established: 2007 (18+ years)
🥥

Betel Nut Plantation

Arecanut is woven into Meghalaya's culture and economy alike. Our commercial-scale cultivation honours that tradition while applying modern agricultural practice.

  • Location: Era-Aning Village, South Garo Hills
  • Area: 1 acre
  • Trees: 2,000+ productive trees
  • Production: 2 tons annually
  • Rank: Meghalaya is 5th nationally in arecanut
🏗️

Government Construction

Roads, community halls, public facilities — built right. We manage government-funded construction with the legal rigour and execution discipline communities deserve.

  • Projects: 2 integrated community halls
  • Location: North Garo Hills
  • Investment: ₹2 Crores
  • Scope: Village & social infrastructure
  • Focus: Community & employment generation
🚛

MNREGA Supply Operations

We are a trusted supply partner for MNREGA-funded rural employment schemes — ensuring materials reach the right places at the right time across Meghalaya.

  • Coverage: 4+ districts of Meghalaya
  • Villages: 4 communities served
  • Materials: Cement & steel reinforcement
  • Purpose: Rural employment & infrastructure
  • Impact: Employment generation & development
